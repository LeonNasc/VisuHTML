About this project

Working on HTML/CSS files can be a nuisance given that most of the time, you'll have to do it by mentally imagining your HTML structure and your CSS styling and then a number of times until you get it right. Because of that, it is very easy to get buried under tons of divs, classes and tags without having any idea of why it is behaving the way it is.

VisuHTML is an WYSIWYG editor that aims to aid anyone who wishes write HTML/CSS to better view their code changes live
